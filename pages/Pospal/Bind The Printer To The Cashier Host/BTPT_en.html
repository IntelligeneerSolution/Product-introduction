Bind Printer to Main POS

November 13, 2024 · Charlie

Navigate to the top-left corner of the front-end POS system

Locate the "Edit Item" option

If you know the item S/N, you can enter it directly. If not, click "Shortcut" to switch to the keyboard

Enter the name of the item to be modified

Select the item you want to modify

Find the "Select Kitchen" option

Choose the kitchen printer for the item

After selecting, click "Save"
